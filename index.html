ONE REPUBLIC

One Republic is an American pop-rock band, founded in Colorado Springs in 2002. It consists of lead vocalist Ryan Tedder, guitarist Zach Filkins, guitarist Drew Brown, bassist and cellist Brent Kutzle, and drummer Eddie Fisher. The band has released 4 albums, has been nominated for the Grammy's, Billboard Music Awards, AMA's, and the Teen Choice Awards, and their songs have charted in the top ten listings of many international countries. One Republic's latest album will be released sometime this year!
